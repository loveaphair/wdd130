Dry Oar Rafting Co.

At Dry Oar Rafting Co., our mission is to provide thrilling, safe, and memorable rafting experiences for adventurers of all skill levels. We believe in fostering a deep connection with nature while navigating the exciting challenges of white water rafting. Our experienced guides are committed to ensuring your safety while helping you create unforgettable memories on some of the most beautiful rivers in the world.

History

Dry Oar Rafting Co. was founded in 1998 by a group of passionate outdoor enthusiasts who wanted to share their love for river rafting with the world. What started as a small operation with just two rafts and a handful of clients has grown into one of the region's premier rafting companies. Over the past two decades, we've safely guided thousands of adventurers down challenging rapids and serene stretches of pristine rivers.

Our company has always prioritized environmental conservation, sustainable practices, and community involvement. We work closely with local conservation groups to maintain the health and beauty of our rivers for future generations to enjoy. Our growth has been steady but mindful, ensuring we maintain the personalized service and attention to detail that has been our hallmark since day one.
